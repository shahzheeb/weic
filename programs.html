WEIC Programs

We have a whole range of programs for all age groups and gender.

Understanding Quran & Islam: 

Every Thursday after Isha by Br. Zafar Ahmed Khan

This program is aimed at improving understanding of Quran and Islam where Br. Zafar go over each Ayah of Quran in detail to explain the meaning and background behind those revelations.

Sunday Khatirah and Breakfast after Fajr: 

We invite a guest speaker every Sunday morning to deliver Khatirah after Fajr prayers followed by breakfast.

Friday Youth Halaqa: 

Every Friday after Isha

Youth Halaqa for middle and high school kids after Isha on Fridays.

Ladies Halaqa: 

Every other Thursday from 10:30 AM to 12:00 PM

Qayam-ul-Lail: 

Every first Saturday of the Month

Adult Tajweed Class: 

By Br. Othman Moaz every Monday 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM.

Seerah of Prophet Mohammed SAW: 

Every Wednesday after Isha.

Sahab Series 

Every Saturday after Isha.


Please contact us at info@westendislamiccenter.org with any questions or feedback about these activities. Jazakum Allahu Khairan.
